The cost of uncertainty

Bad subsurface assumptions scale fast.

In mining and environmental projects, every decision compounds on what's assumed to be beneath the surface. A poorly integrated hydrogeological dataset, an outdated geological model, or a disconnected GIS layer can translate into months of rework, misallocated CAPEX, or permitting delays worth millions.

Most operators don't lack data — they lack the specialized hand to integrate, interpret and model it at the level their decisions demand. That's where we come in.

Delayed decisions
Teams stuck waiting on a geologist to reconcile conflicting datasets.
Hidden risk
Models that don't reflect recent drilling or updated structural interpretations.
Tool sprawl
Leapfrog, ArcGIS, QGIS, Surpac — nobody stitching outputs into a coherent picture.
Why projects with us resolve differently

Most consultancies subcontract parts of the work. We keep field, lab and data under one roof — which is why our answers show up sooner, hold up under audit, and don’t unravel when someone asks “where does this number come from?”

— One team

Field, lab and data in a single team.

Drilling logs, hydro samples and GIS layers don’t travel through a chain of subcontractors before they hit the model. Fewer handoffs, fewer translation errors, tighter timelines.

— Auditable

Every result, traceable to its source.

When required, we implement blockchain-based data traceability across the report chain — so clients can verify that a number was never silently altered between the field and the final deliverable.

— Speed + certainty

More speed, less uncertainty.

Implicit 3D modeling in Leapfrog paired with our own processing tooling lets us deliver sharper answers in less time — especially on projects where drilling, structural and hydro datasets all need to reconcile.
